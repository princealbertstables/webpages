Welcome

 Prince Albert Stables

Where the breeding, training, and selling of top quality Rocky Mountain Horses
is both our business and our passion.
We strive to bring to the market a horse that meets the needs of the individual owner.
Our horses are selectively bred to so as to continue the desired characteristics that have made
this breed so popular and much in demand. With our staff of trainers and caretakers
we carefully prepare the horse so that their eventual owners will learn, as we have, that the
temperament, intelligence, and athleticism of the Rocky Mountain Horse are
unsurpassed by any other breed of horse.
